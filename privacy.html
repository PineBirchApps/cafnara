Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 May 2026

Cafnara is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains, in plain language, what data the app handles and how.

What data Cafnara stores

Cafnara stores the data you create as you use the app:

  • Coffee and caffeine entries you log (drink, amount of caffeine, date and time).
  • Your custom drinks and quick-add preferences.
  • App settings such as daily limit, sleep-safe threshold, bedtime, absorption delay, and caffeine half-life.
  • Onboarding state and a launch counter used to time the in-app paywall fairly.
  • If you purchase Premium, a record of which product you bought and the entitlement status reported by the App Store.

Where your data lives

All of the above is stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you are signed in to iCloud, the same data syncs through Apple's CloudKit using your own private container. The developer of Cafnara has no access to that container and cannot read your entries, settings, or purchase history.

If you sign out of iCloud, disable iCloud for Cafnara, or delete the app, the synced copy is handled by Apple according to your iCloud settings. The local copy is removed when you delete the app.

What Cafnara does not do

  • No analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, no third-party trackers.
  • No accounts, no passwords, no email collection inside the app.
  • No sale or sharing of personal data with third parties.
  • No background location access. Cafnara does not request your location.

Purchases

Premium is sold through Apple's App Store using StoreKit. When you make a purchase, Apple handles the transaction. Cafnara only receives the entitlement information needed to unlock features (such as which product was purchased and whether the subscription is active). Apple's privacy policy applies to the payment itself.

Diagnostics

Cafnara does not send diagnostic data to the developer. If you opt in to Apple's standard "Share with App Developers" setting in iOS, Apple may share aggregated, anonymised crash and performance data. That flow is controlled entirely by iOS and you can disable it at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

Children

Cafnara is intended for adults who track their own caffeine intake. It is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, the updated date at the top will change and the new version will be published on this page.

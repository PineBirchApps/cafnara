Track your caffeine. Sleep better.

Cafnara is a private, local-first caffeine tracker for iOS. Log a drink in a tap, watch active caffeine fade in real time, and stay below your sleep-safe limit.

Download on the App Store Need help?

One-tap logging

Quick-add cards for the drinks you actually have. Reorder, hide, or add your own custom coffees.

Live caffeine estimate

Half-life modelling shows what is still active in your system right now, not just what you drank.

Sleep-safe limit

Set a bedtime and a personal sensitivity. Cafnara warns you when a late coffee is likely to bite.

Trends that make sense

Week, month, and year views with averages, late-coffee insights, and your typical last cup time.

Private iCloud sync

Your history syncs across your devices through your private iCloud. Nothing goes to our servers.

Designed for iOS 26

Native SwiftUI, Dynamic Type, full localisation in 11 languages, and a calm coffee-toned interface.

Privacy by default

Cafnara stores your data on your device. If you are signed in to iCloud, it syncs through your own private CloudKit container — the developer cannot read it. There is no analytics SDK, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

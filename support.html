Frequently asked

My entries are not syncing between my iPhone and iPad.

Cafnara syncs through your private iCloud. Make sure both devices are signed in to the same Apple ID, that iCloud Drive is on, and that Cafnara is enabled under Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud. The first sync after install can take a few minutes.

How does the active caffeine estimate work?

Cafnara models caffeine using a half-life curve. After you log a drink, an absorption delay is applied, then the amount decays based on the half-life you set in Settings. Lower half-life means caffeine clears faster; higher means it lingers.

How do I change my sleep-safe limit or bedtime?

Open the Settings tab. You can adjust your bedtime, daily caffeine limit, sleep-safe threshold, absorption delay, and caffeine half-life. Tap the small info icon next to any row for an explanation.

I purchased Premium but I don't see the features.

In the app, open the paywall and tap Restore Purchases. This asks the App Store to re-check your entitlement. If it still doesn't appear, email us with the Apple ID region you purchased from and we'll help.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions and choose Cafnara to change or cancel. Lifetime purchases are one-time and never charge again.

Can I export my caffeine history?

Not yet — it's on the roadmap. If this would be useful for you, let us know what format you'd want (CSV, Apple Health, JSON) so we can prioritise.

How do I delete all my data?

Deleting Cafnara from your device removes the local data. To remove the synced copy, go to Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Cafnara and choose Delete Data from iCloud.
